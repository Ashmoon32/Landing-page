Leonardo da Vinci , Renaissance Polymath

Leonardo da Vinci (1452–1519) was an Italian polymath whose genius spanned art, science, engineering, anatomy, and invention. Renowned for masterpieces like Mona Lisa and The Last Supper, he combined deep observation with innovative thinking, leaving behind notebooks filled with visionary ideas centuries ahead of his time. His insatiable curiosity and relentless pursuit of knowledge made him one of history’s most extraordinary minds.
